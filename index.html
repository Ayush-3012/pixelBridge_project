How it Works

Odon Finance is a blockchain platform built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) and Moonriver network with the mission of maximizing the full potential of DeFi and it is the first decentralized platform that rewards course participants via StudyFi program Learn to Earn. Beside "Learn and Earn platform", Odon Finance providing high-quality inclusive financial services including liquidity mining, staking, decentralized exchange (DEX), non-custodial wallet, bridge between chains (Cross-Chain) and NFT marketplace (under development).

We’ve built a platform to maximize your finance and grow your knowledge.

We make it easier for everyone to start earning passive income through DeFi, without the complexities.

  • Buy or Sell ODON directly with one single click on our decentralized exchange. Anytime.
